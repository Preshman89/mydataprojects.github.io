    Emergency Room visit Analysis using Microsoft Excel

    This project was unique as it aimed at establishing the average wait time of ER patient. After so much compalains about delays at the ER center which resulted in a very low patient satisfaction score, The management sort to find the cause of the delays and the analysis of the patients data showed even the hourly patient arrival to the ER identifying the need to have more providers avaliable to meet the demands. Upon implementation of the insights, patients wait time was reduced by 40% and the average satisfaction score increased
